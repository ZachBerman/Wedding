Getting Here

We are aware that most of you will have to fly to get to Golden, and we are so grateful you will be joining us for our special day. Perhaps the easiest and most economical way to get here is to fly to Calgary International Airport, which offers many daily flights from the USA for reasonable rates. From there, you will either need to rent a car or take a shuttle. The Canadian Greyhound line is actually quite nice, affordable, and has free wireless and has approximately 3 daily shuttles to Golden from Calgary. The ride is about 3-4 hours on the bus. There is a public train and a Greyhound bus from the airport to the Greyhound Terminal. There is also a shuttle bus that will take you from the airport to the terminal for $10 CAD..

Vacation Rentals

There are lots of rentals in the Town of Golden, and up on the mountain near Kicking Horse Ski Area (20 minute drive)

VRBO Rentals

Condos at Kicking Horse Ski Area

KHMR is willing to block off groups of 1-2 BD condos at the ski area and offer a 10% discount if you are booking for our wedding weekend. Both the Glacier and Mountaineer lodges are right at the base area if you want to take the gondola up while you are here for mountain biking, hiking, or to do the via feratta!

There are large variety of condos available, some better for couples, some for groups with children, etc. Some of the VRBO and AirBNB rentals are up at the ski hill to, the internet has a lot of options!
