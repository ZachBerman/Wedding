So you're coming!

We are so grateful you will be joining us to celebrate our special day. Perhaps the easiest, most economical way to get here is to fly to Calgary International Airport (YYC). The airport has many daily flights from the US for reasonable rates.

From there...

  • Rent a car and drive the Trans-Canada! Takes approximately 2 hours and 45 minutes.
  • Take an airport shuttle or the public transit train to the Greyhound bus line which has 3 daily shuttles to Golden from downtown Calgary.
  • Take a shuttle to Golden from the airport (a little more expensive).

Condos at Kicking Horse Ski Area

Kicking Horse Mountain Resort will block off condos for our group, offering 1, 2, and 3 bedroom suites at the Glacier Lodge. Wedding party guests will receive 10% off base rates. You can also keep your eyes open for sales in the building which happen frequently. This is a fun area to stay in, because the gondola and bike park are right outside the building, for easy hiking, biking, or accessing the via ferrata and the Eagle's Eye restaurant on top of the mountain!

If you want someone to chat with about your options, you can email our favorite front desk gal' Katie Diddams, who manages the Glacier Lodge - kh.gc@bellstar.ca
